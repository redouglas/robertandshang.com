The Lodge at Malibou Lake is an historic venue in Agoura Hills, Southern California. Hidden in the rustic Santa Monica Mountains, shaded by sycamore and oak trees, and beside a storied private lake.

The Lodge is located less than an hour from downtown Los Angeles, minutes from the 101 freeway, and a step back in time from the pace of modern life.

FAQ

  • Do I have to RSVP?
    Yep! Absolutely. We need to know who's coming so that we can plan an amazing evening for everyone. Thanks for understanding.
  • What is the RSVP deadline?
    April 21, 2019. After that we will assume you will NOT be attending.
  • I have some RSVP questions or concerns...
    Just call or email us directly.
  • I think you mean "Malibu".
    Nope, it's actually  Malibou Lake.
  • When should I arrive?
    Please arrive before 5:00pm. Ceremony will begin promptly at 5pm.
  • When does it end?
    The event will end promptly at 11:00pm.
  • Will there be parking at the venue?
    Yes there will be free parking at the venue. Guests staying at the hotel can leave their cars there and make use of the shuttle bus.
  • Can I bring a plus one?
    Yes, you may bring one guest. Be sure to fill in their name on the RSVP form. Please let us know if this changes after you have RSVPed.
  • Can I bring our kids?
    No. Because of limitations from the venue and to create a more relaxed atmosphere for our guests, only kids from close family will be present. Thanks for understanding.
  • What should I expect?
    Short outdoor wedding ceremony, cocktail hour, dinner, open bar, dancing & games.
  • What's for dinner?
    We are serving food family style (menu TBD). We hope that this will help bring everyone together while also allowing people to be more flexible with how much, and what they eat. Table to table bartering is encouraged. If you have any dietary restrictions at all it is important that you let us know in your RSVP.
  • Dress code?
    Cocktail attire please, but first and foremost, be comfortable and enjoy yourself. White clothing is reserved for the bride. The ceremony will be outdoors on a grass lawn, so wear appropriate shoes. The reception and cocktail hour will be held indoors in the adjoining lodge. We recommend bringing a light jacket for the evening, just in case.
  • Can I take pictures?
    We ask that guests please refrain from using their phones or taking pictures/videos during the ceremony to keep the moment peaceful and allow our photographers to work their magic. After that, all photos are welcome and encouraged. There will also be a photobooth.
  • Do you have a hashtag for the social media?
    #GaoDownTheAisle
  • Is there a gift registry?
    We're just happy you're celebrating with us! But we have also registered  here.
